Gaminguild, previously TotalWeb Inc was a It company offering Web solutions. Fed up with the existent IT practices, TotalWeb was started by three engineers as a garage project, TotalWeb's motive was to offer the clients, a business experience free of technical jargon, unreasonably long SLAs, unreal clauses and keeping up with constantly changing unapathetic staff. Read more about our motive and business model.

Gaminguild, which started as one of the many innovative side projects at TotalWeb is a platform to produce superior learning management tools by gamifying education. Learn more about the Story of TotalWeb and inception and objective of gaminguild and the projects of Gaminguild.
With the collective decision taken by the investors, advisors, management and staff, Gaminguild was made the core of operations at TotalWeb and it was decided to rebrand the company as Gaminguild to reflect the newly adopted policies in a reverse merger of sorts.
Gaminguild continues to support the projects commissioned for TotalWeb and has been has making sure to go the extra mile to ensure seamless transition for everyone involved.
The TotalWeb projects wing operates independently within Gaminguild to make sure that there's room for any potential conflict of interest and resources between Gaminguild projects and the client projects. Have a look at our TotalWeb Projects wing.
